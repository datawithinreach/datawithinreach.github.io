Data Within Reach Lab

We are the Data Within Reach lab in the computer science department at Boston College. As our world is becoming increasingly data-driven, the ability to understand and communicate data is becoming an essential skill for everyone. Our research vision is to make data more accessible and bring it within reach of broader audiences from diverse backgrounds. We aim to inform the world about the best data visualization practice and investigate novel approaches to empower people to interact with data beyond traditional expert analytic systems.
